Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When and where will FusionTechathon take place?

A: FusionTechathon starts at 8am in the morning and ends on the same day at 8pm at Alvas Engineering and Technology Campus, Moodbidri, Mangalore_574425.

Q: Who can take part in this FusionTechathon?

A: FusionTechathon is open only for the students who are pursuing engineering of any year/sem. All students belong to this category are welcome to take part in this event.

Q: What is the duration of FusionTechathon?

A: FusionTechathon typically spans of 12 hours, during which participants work on their projects and engage with their fellow participants.

Q: Can participate as solo?

A: Absolutely not, participants are encouraged to form teams to foster collaboration and diverse skillsets. Teams typically consist of 2-4 members.

Q: What are the benefits of participating?

A: Participating in FusionTechathon provides a chance to network with fellow tech enthusiasts, gain exposure to industry experts, showcase your skills, and even win exciting prizes.

Q:Will food be provided during the event?

A: Yes, we've got your sustenance covered! Refreshments and delicious meals will be provided throughout the event, ensuring you stay energized and focused on your innovative endeavors.

Is accommodation available for participants?

A: Participants will have the option to avail accommodation during the FusionTechathon event. However, please note that accommodation will be offered on a charged basis.
